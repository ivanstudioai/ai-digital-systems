COLLABORATION MODES

This file defines common patterns of interaction between humans and AI
during knowledge work.

A collaboration mode describes how the human and AI divide roles and
how the interaction is structured.

---

MODE: Co-Pilot

Description:
The AI assists the human in producing work by generating drafts,
suggestions, or variations while the human guides direction.

Human Role:
Direct the task, provide intent, review outputs, and refine results.

AI Role:
Generate drafts, expand ideas, and propose alternative approaches.

Typical Use Cases:

* Draft writing
* Brainstorming ideas
* Content creation
* Exploring alternatives

---

MODE: Challenger

Description:
The AI challenges assumptions, identifies weaknesses, and provides
critical perspectives on the human's thinking.

Human Role:
Present ideas, arguments, or plans for examination.

AI Role:
Question assumptions, highlight risks, and provide counterarguments.

Typical Use Cases:

* Strategic decisions
* Argument testing
* Risk analysis
* Decision preparation

---

MODE: Synthesizer

Description:
The AI integrates multiple ideas, sources, or perspectives into
coherent structures or explanations.

Human Role:
Provide materials, perspectives, or ideas to combine.

AI Role:
Organize information, connect concepts, and produce structured outputs.

Typical Use Cases:

* Research synthesis
* Insight generation
* Concept integration
* Multi-source summaries

---

MODE: Reducer

Description:
The AI simplifies complex or large information into shorter,
clearer formats while preserving key meaning.

Human Role:
Provide the source material and define the desired level of reduction.

AI Role:
Condense, summarize, and highlight key points.

Typical Use Cases:

* Document summaries
* Executive briefs
* Simplified explanations
* Key insight extraction

---

MODE: Auditor

Description:
The AI reviews work for issues, inconsistencies, or weaknesses.

Human Role:
Produce the primary work and request review.

AI Role:
Check logic, clarity, and completeness.

Typical Use Cases:

* Draft review
* Logic checking
* Quality assurance
* Consistency verification
