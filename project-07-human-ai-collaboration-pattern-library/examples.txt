EXAMPLES

This file demonstrates how the Human–AI Collaboration Pattern Library
can be applied to real-world knowledge work scenarios.

Each example follows the collaboration blueprint format.

---

=== COLLABORATION BLUEPRINT ===

Scenario:
Startup Product Strategy

Task Type:
Strategic

Recommended Modes:
Challenger
Synthesizer

Human Role:
Define the product vision, market context, and strategic goals.

AI Role:
Challenge assumptions about the market and synthesize possible
strategic approaches.

Interaction Structure:

1. Human outlines product idea and target market.
2. AI challenges assumptions and identifies risks.
3. AI synthesizes alternative strategic options.
4. Human evaluates options and refines direction.

Risks:
AI may produce plausible but unrealistic strategic suggestions.

Limitations:
AI does not have real market validation or operational constraints.

=== COLLABORATION BLUEPRINT ===

Scenario:
Technical Research Summary

Task Type:
Reduction

Recommended Modes:
Reducer

Human Role:
Provide the research papers or reports and specify the desired level
of summarization.

AI Role:
Condense the material into a clear and structured summary.

Interaction Structure:

1. Human provides research material.
2. AI extracts key concepts and findings.
3. AI produces a concise summary.
4. Human verifies accuracy and completeness.

Risks:
Important nuance may be lost during summarization.

Limitations:
AI may misinterpret specialized terminology.

=== COLLABORATION BLUEPRINT ===

Scenario:
Marketing Campaign Idea Generation

Task Type:
Generative

Recommended Modes:
Co-Pilot
Auditor

Human Role:
Define campaign goals, audience, and constraints.

AI Role:
Generate creative campaign ideas and review them for clarity
and effectiveness.

Interaction Structure:

1. Human defines campaign objective.
2. AI generates multiple campaign ideas.
3. Human selects promising concepts.
4. AI reviews and refines selected ideas.

Risks:
AI may produce generic or repetitive ideas.

Limitations:
AI lacks deep understanding of brand identity or audience nuance.

=== COLLABORATION BLUEPRINT ===

Scenario:
Competitive Product Analysis

Task Type:
Analytical

Recommended Modes:
Synthesizer
Auditor

Human Role:
Provide data on competitor products and define analysis goals.

AI Role:
Organize the information into structured comparisons and check
for analytical consistency.

Interaction Structure:

1. Human provides product data.
2. AI synthesizes comparisons between competitors.
3. AI identifies patterns and insights.
4. Human verifies conclusions and adjusts interpretation.

Risks:
AI may overgeneralize patterns from limited data.

Limitations:
AI cannot independently verify real-world product performance.

=== COLLABORATION BLUEPRINT ===

Scenario:
Design Proposal Review

Task Type:
Evaluative

Recommended Modes:
Challenger
Auditor

Human Role:
Present the design proposal and evaluation criteria.

AI Role:
Critically examine the proposal and identify weaknesses or
inconsistencies.

Interaction Structure:

1. Human presents design proposal.
2. AI challenges assumptions and highlights potential issues.
3. AI reviews logical consistency and completeness.
4. Human decides which critiques are relevant.

Risks:
AI critiques may be overly theoretical.

Limitations:
AI lacks real stakeholder context.
