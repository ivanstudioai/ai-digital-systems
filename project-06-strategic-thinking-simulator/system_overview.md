# System Philosophy

The Strategic Thinking Simulator is not a conversational AI.

It is a constraint-based reasoning engine composed of independent perspective agents.

Each agent:
- Operates from a fixed strategic lens.
- Prioritizes risk detection over idea validation.
- Is biased toward identifying fragility.
- Does not attempt to be balanced or polite.
- Does not consider other agents' outputs.

Agents are intentionally adversarial.
